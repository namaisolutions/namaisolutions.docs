# Privacy Policy for Bike Clinic Pro

**Effective Date:** June 17, 2026

NamAiSolutions built the Bike Clinic Pro app as a Commercial/Free app. This SERVICE is provided by us and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

## 1. Offline-First Data Storage

Bike Clinic Pro is an **offline-first application**. This means that all data you enter into the app—including customer names, phone numbers, vehicle details, service history, and billing information—is stored **locally on your device** using an internal SQLite database. 

We **do not** transmit, store, or process your data on any remote servers. You have complete control over your data.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. Because the app operates offline, this information is retained on your device and is not collected by us in any way.

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, specifically the underlying mobile operating system (Android) and app store distribution platforms (Google Play).

## 3. App Permissions

To provide core functionality, Bike Clinic Pro requests the following device permissions:

*   **Camera:** We request camera access to allow you to capture photos of vehicles, job cards, and bills. These photos are saved locally to your device and linked to your service records.
*   **Storage (Read/Write External Storage):** We require storage permissions to allow you to select existing photos from your gallery, and generate and save PDF invoices. 

None of the files, photos, or documents accessed or generated by these permissions are uploaded to our servers.

## 4. Security and Data Protection

We value your trust in providing us your Data. Because the app operates entirely offline and stores data locally, the security of your data relies on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level security features (such as screen locks and device encryption) to protect your data. Because we do not have access to your device or data, we cannot recover any lost data.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
